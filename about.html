About ENIGMA

ENIGMA is not just a cultural fest, it is the heartbeat of UGI's creativity, energy and student spirit. Every year, ENIGMA brings together students from all branches, all talents and all backgrounds to celebrate what defines us as a community: art, expression, passion, and unity.

Over the years, ENIGMA has grown into one of the most awaited festivals on campus, featuring everything from high energy dance performances, soulful music and dramatic acts to intense sports competitions, creative challenges and vibrant cultural showcases.

This year marks the 13th edition of ENIGMA, taking place on 21 and 22 November 2025. With new ideas, bigger stages and a wide range of events, we are ready to deliver an experience that is louder, brighter and more memorable than ever before.

ENIGMA is where friendships are built, memories are created, talents shine, and the entire college comes together as one.

Join us for two days of culture, creativity, and unforgettable moments. Welcome to ENIGMA.
