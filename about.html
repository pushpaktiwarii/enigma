About ENIGMA XIII

ENIGMA XIII is more than just a festival; it's a celebration of creativity, talent, and the vibrant spirit of our college community. For over a decade, ENIGMA has been the cornerstone of our cultural calendar, bringing together students from all disciplines to showcase their artistic prowess, competitive spirit, and collaborative energy.

This year, as we celebrate our thirteenth edition, we continue to push boundaries and "Expect the Unexpected." From electrifying dance performances to thought-provoking literary competitions, from high-energy sports events to innovative creative challenges, ENIGMA XIII promises an unforgettable experience.

Join us on November 21-22, 2025, as we create memories, forge friendships, and celebrate the diverse talents that make our college community truly special.
